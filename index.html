What is WishFunder?

WishFunder helps digital creators monetize their audience through one-time and recurring support. Fans make payments via trusted payment providers, while creators manage their earnings through a simple dashboard.

How It Works

1. Fan Support

Fans tip creators using cards and local payment methods provided by licensed processors.

2. Platform Accounting

WishFunder records transactions and allocates creator balances.

3. Creator Payout Requests

Creators request payouts based on available balance and supported regions.

4. Settlement

Payouts are processed externally through supported payout rails.

Security & Compliance

WishFunder does not store card data or process payments directly. All payments are handled by PCI-compliant partners.

Creator features and payouts are available only in supported regions and are subject to applicable regulations.

For Payment Partners

• Platform model: digital creator monetization
• Revenue: platform fee per transaction
• Payments: fully delegated to licensed processors
• No custody of customer card data

WishFunder

A global platform where fans support creators through tips and donations. All payments are processed by licensed payment providers.

How It Works

  • Fans support creators using trusted third-party payment processors
  • WishFunder records contributions and platform fees
  • Creators may request payouts via supported methods
  • No card data is ever stored on WishFunder servers

Security & Compliance

  • PCI-compliant payment providers handle all card payments
  • Fraud monitoring and abuse prevention systems in place
  • No custody of user payment credentials

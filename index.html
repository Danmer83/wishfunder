About WishFunder

WishFunder is a global creator monetization platform that allows fans to support digital creators via one-time or recurring donations. All payments are processed through licensed payment providers.

How It Works

1. Fans Donate

Supporters make payments using trusted global payment processors.

2. Platform Allocation

WishFunder records donations and allocates creator earnings.

3. Creator Payouts

Creators request payouts via cryptocurrency or supported payout partners.

4. Transparency

Creators access a dashboard with earnings and payout history.

Security & Compliance

WishFunder does not store or process card data. All payments are handled by PCI-compliant payment providers. The platform follows industry-standard security practices.
